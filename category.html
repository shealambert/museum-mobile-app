Museum Collection

Travel back in time to when the carousel was the pinnacle of amusement park joy. Now on view, over 100 years of carousel art and history are on view in our Main Gallery and throughout our 33,000 square foot building.

Restored Collection

Our skilled restoration team, who has learned and practiced the proper craft and techniques of carousels, carefully restores worn down carousels. They receive carousels in rough conditions and return fully repaired and freshly painted, providing carousels with a second life.
